India Fintech Market Overview 2025 to 2026

India is the third largest fintech market globally with over 10000 registered fintech startups as of 2025.
The Indian fintech sector attracted USD 8.9 billion in investments in 2024 and is projected to reach USD 420 billion in transaction value by 2026.
UPI processed over 18 billion transactions in December 2024 alone, crossing INR 23 lakh crore in monthly value.

Key segments driving growth:
- Digital lending platforms like Navi, KreditBee, and MoneyView disbursed over 5 crore loans in FY2024.
- Buy Now Pay Later adoption grew 43 percent year on year driven by Gen Z consumers.
- Wealth management platforms like Groww and Zerodha crossed 100 million registered users combined.
- Insurance tech saw 38 percent growth with players like Digit and Acko expanding into rural markets.

Regulatory landscape:
RBI introduced the Digital Lending Guidelines in 2023 which mandated direct disbursement to borrower accounts.
SEBI approved the use of AI for investment advisory with mandatory disclosure requirements from 2024.
The Account Aggregator framework now has over 1.1 billion linked accounts enabling consent based data sharing.

Major players by segment:
Payments: PhonePe 48 percent UPI market share, Google Pay 37 percent, Paytm declining to 8 percent after RBI action.
Lending: Bajaj Finance, Navi, KreditBee, Slice, LazyPay.
Wealth: Zerodha, Groww, Angel One, Upstox.
Insurance: PolicyBazaar, Acko, Digit Insurance.
