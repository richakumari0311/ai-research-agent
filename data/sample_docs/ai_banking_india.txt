AI in Indian Banking: Real Deployments 2024 to 2025

ICICI Bank AI Initiatives:
ICICI Bank deployed an AI model called iLens for real time loan underwriting.
iLens processes over 50 data points including GST history, bank statement analysis, and bureau scores.
Loan approval time reduced from 3 days to under 2 hours for MSME borrowers.
The model uses gradient boosting with SHAP values for explainability to meet RBI audit requirements.

Axis Bank Credit Risk AI:
Axis Bank partnered with Experian to build an alternative credit scoring model for thin file customers.
The model uses UPI transaction patterns, mobile recharge history, and utility bill payments as features.
Approval rates for new to credit customers improved by 28 percent without increasing default rates.
The system processes 200000 applications per month in real time.

State Bank of India Digital Transformation:
SBI launched YONO platform which now has over 70 million registered users.
AI chatbot SBI Intelligent Assistant handles 3 million queries per month with 87 percent resolution rate.
Fraud detection system flags 94 percent of fraudulent transactions before they are processed.
ML models scan 2 billion transactions monthly for anomaly detection.

RBI Regulatory Technology Initiatives:
RBI launched a regulatory sandbox for fintech companies to test AI products in a controlled environment.
Over 80 fintech companies have participated across cohorts focused on lending, payments, and fraud prevention.
RBI mandated that all AI models used in credit decisioning must be explainable and auditable from April 2024.
Banks must maintain model risk management frameworks and conduct quarterly model validation reviews.

Key AI Tools Used by Indian Banks:
- Python with scikit-learn and XGBoost for credit scoring models
- Apache Kafka for real time transaction streaming
- AWS SageMaker and Azure ML for model training and deployment
- Vector databases like Pinecone and ChromaDB for document search
- LangChain and RAG pipelines for internal knowledge management
- MLflow for experiment tracking and model versioning
