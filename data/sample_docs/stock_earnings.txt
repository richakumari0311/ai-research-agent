Indian Stock Market and Earnings Intelligence Q4 FY2025

HDFC Bank Q4 FY2025 Earnings Summary:
Net Interest Income grew 9.2 percent year on year to INR 29646 crore.
Net Profit came in at INR 17622 crore, up 6.7 percent year on year, slightly below analyst estimate of INR 18200 crore.
Gross NPA improved to 1.24 percent from 1.42 percent in Q3 FY2025.
Management guided for loan growth of 12 to 14 percent in FY2026.
Key concern: deposit growth at 14.1 percent is lagging loan growth of 16.4 percent.

Infosys Q4 FY2025 Earnings Summary:
Revenue grew 8.9 percent year on year in constant currency terms to USD 4.94 billion.
Net profit rose 11.8 percent to INR 7969 crore.
Company raised FY2026 revenue growth guidance to 4.5 to 6.5 percent.
Large deal wins of USD 2.5 billion with 65 percent being net new deals.
GenAI related revenues now contributing to 7 percent of total deal pipeline.

Reliance Industries Q4 FY2025 Earnings Summary:
Consolidated revenue at INR 2.63 lakh crore, up 7.3 percent year on year.
EBITDA at INR 47136 crore driven by retail and digital segments.
Jio Platforms added 18 million net subscribers taking total to 489 million.
Retail segment crossed INR 100000 crore annual EBITDA milestone for first time.
O2C segment faced pressure due to weak global refining margins.

Nifty 50 Performance FY2025:
Nifty 50 delivered 12.4 percent returns in FY2025 after a weak Q3.
Midcap and smallcap indices outperformed with 24 and 31 percent returns respectively.
Foreign institutional investors net bought USD 12.3 billion in Indian equities in FY2025.
Top performing sectors: Capital Goods, Defence, Real Estate, Consumer Discretionary.
Underperforming sectors: IT Services, FMCG, Metals.
